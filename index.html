Web Design

A business without a website is hardly a business it all. We create beautiful website designs that are functional, mobile responsive and easy to use. Your website should not only look good, but actually promote your business in the fullest sense of the word.

WordPress

WordPress was first launched in 2003. At that time, it was seen as a fairly simple CMS for blogging purposes. Even as recently as a few years ago, many Web designers still thought of WordPress as a CMS best suited to blogs. Now, however, this has changed. WordPress is capable of acting as the backbone for all kinds of different websites. It’s now used by names as big as Samsung, Forbes and CNN.

Analytics

Google Analytics is a free tool that tracks information about the way visitors to your site interact with it. You will be able to see how much traffic each keyword brings to your website. Analytics will open up a world of information you probably never knew about visitors to your site.
